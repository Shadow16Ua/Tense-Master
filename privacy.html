Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TenseMaster. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we look after your data when you visit our website.

2. Data We Collect

TenseMaster is a static educational platform. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal identification information (like your name or email) unless you voluntarily contact us via email.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage strictly for functional purposes to enhance your experience:

  • To remember your selected language preference.
  • To remember your preferred theme (Dark/Light mode).
  • To save your progress on grammar tabs.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that harvest your personal data.

4. Third-Party Links

This website may include links to third-party websites or resources. Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at Contact Page.

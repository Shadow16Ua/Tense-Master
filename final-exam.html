The Final Exam

24 questions. All 12 tenses. Mixed order. Green means correct, red means incorrect. Good luck!

1. I ___ (see) him at the mall yesterday.

2. She ___ (already / finish) her homework.

3. At 8 PM tomorrow, we ___ (have) dinner.

4. Why ___ (you / cry)? What happened?

5. By the time I arrived, the train ___ (leave).

6. I think it ___ (rain) tomorrow.

7. He usually ___ (wake up) at 7 AM.

8. While I ___ (cook), the phone rang.

9. By next year, I ___ (save) enough money.

10. I ___ (wait) for you for two hours!

11. He was tired because he ___ (work) all day.

12. By December, she ___ (live) here for 3 years.

13. Water ___ (freeze) at 0 degrees Celsius.

14. We ___ (not / go) to the beach last weekend.

15. Don't call me at 9 PM, I ___ (watch) a movie.

16. Look! The bus ___ (come).

17. I couldn't pay because I ___ (lose) my wallet.

18. I promise I ___ (not / tell) anyone.

19. ___ (you / ever / be) to Japan?

20. They ___ (play) tennis when it started to rain.

21. By 5 PM, the mechanic ___ (fix) the car.

22. How long ___ (you / learn) English?

23. The ground was wet because it ___ (snow) all night.

24. By the time we arrive, they ___ (play) for an hour.
